The problem

US bank failures are rare and clustered — the 2008–2012 credit wave, then the 2023 rate-driven runs. The job of an early-warning model is to rank the few at-risk banks out of thousands, quarters ahead, without seeing the future.

Live stress-test lab

Real-time inference against the deployed model. Test a real institution and fact-check the prediction against what actually happened, or build a hypothetical bank and watch it score.
